To the Moon!


How to play: Bet an amount of points and confirm this value by pressing the 'BET' button. This will take from your total amount immediately. After betting, hit the 'START' button to start the game.
When the game starts, the multiplyer will start to go up until it will eventually crash at a random value. Use the 'CASH OUT' button to earn your rewards before the game crashes! If you fail to cash out before it crashes, you lose your betted points. Have fun!
